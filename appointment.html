Your Appointment

A private, one-on-one fitting — we are glad to welcome you.

What to expect

This is an unhurried, one-on-one fitting — just you and your tailor. We’ll read your body and posture, talk through the founding collection and the cloths, and take the precise measurements your suit is built from. There is nothing to prepare beyond the two notes below. Allow about an hour.

What to bring

  • A suit or jacket you already love. Bring a piece that fits you the way you wish everything did — we’ll use it as a reference for cut, length and proportion.

What to wear

  • Fitted, form-following clothing. A close layer lets us measure accurately. Loose or bulky clothing makes a true reading difficult.
