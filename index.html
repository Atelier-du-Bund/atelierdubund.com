The Founding Collection

Four fits. One standard.

Our first chapter is small and deliberate — a limited founding round. Every suit begins as one of four fits, two for men and two for women, then is tailored to you. “Fit” is the cut: tailored or relaxed.

The Cloth

Chosen by hand, made for one.

Fine wool and wool-blend cloths, cut to your measurements and finished by hand in the Shanghai–Hong Kong tradition. The cloth, the buttons, the lining — chosen with you, in person.

Every suit is finished with a silk-touch lining — with a pure-silk upgrade available — and carries a complimentary embroidered signature.

Heritage

From the Bund to the Saint-Lawrence.

The Hongbang — the “Red Gang” tailors — are a lineage, not a label. From a coastal beginning they became the master tailors of old Shanghai, carried their craft to Hong Kong, and now bring it to Montréal.

Shanghai — the Bund, 1930s

On the Bund, the Red Gang became the master tailors of 1930s Shanghai — cutting suits with a precision that defined an era of glamour.
