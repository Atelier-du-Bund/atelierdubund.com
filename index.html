The Atelier

A Private Affair

There are no showrooms and no racks. Each suit begins by invitation — a single appointment, in a private space downtown, hosted by the founder herself. For an afternoon, the atelier is yours alone.

The Fitting

The Fitting

An experienced seamstress takes your measurements by hand — unhurried, precise, the way it has always been done. Then we move through it together: the cloth, the cut, the lining, and the small private details — the colour of a thread, the turn of a cuff, a monogram known only to you.
