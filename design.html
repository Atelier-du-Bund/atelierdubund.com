Design

Your suit is designed
in person.

There is no configurator to click through at midnight. Every Atelier du Bund commission is composed at your rendez-vous in Montréal — cloth in hand, choices made across the table, against the suit you already love.

Reserve a Design Appointment
The Rendez-vous

Considered, not clicked.

Anyone can offer three hundred fabrics and an algorithm. We offer a tight, deliberate selection and an hour of undivided attention — so the decisions feel like the start of a relationship, not a checkout.

When you arrive, we walk the menu below together. Nothing here is final until it’s decided in the room.

Like to come prepared? Sketch a starting brief in the Design Studio

The House Menu

Every choice we’ll make together.

A preview of what your design appointment covers — from silhouette to the smallest red stitch.

01 · Silhouette

The Fit

Maximilian · Edmund · Josephine · Maggie — tailored or relaxed, men’s or women’s, as your starting line.

02 · Cloth & Colour

The Cloth

Wool blend with stretch in black, beige, blue or white. Standard or Premium construction.

03 · Lapel

The Line

Notch, peak or shawl — and how wide it sits across the chest.

04 · Buttoning

The Front

Single-breasted one- or two-button, or a double-breasted wrap.

05 · Pockets & Vents

The Detail

Flap, jetted or patch pockets; side vents, a single centre vent, or none.

06 · Lining

The Secret

Matched, or the house’s signature oxblood red — with jade, gold and ink also at hand.

07 · Trousers

The Base

Flat-front or pleated; plain hem or turned cuff; cut to your chosen silhouette.

08 · Finishing

The Signature

Working surgeon’s cuffs, oxblood or horn buttons, pick-stitch, a discreet monogram, a boutonnière hole.

Then —

We Build It

Measured, sourced and hand-finished, then fitted to you in Montréal.
